Con-x: Network. Innovation. Partnership.

At TravelgateX, we have been connecting travel businesses for the past 6 years. Online. We think it's time for our Partners to connect face to face.

Welcome to Con X, a one day conference specially designed for you to meet & connect with all those Partners who help you multiply your business, and also to see all those friendly faces that you already know again.

Come and join us in the beautiful island of Mallorca - the Travel Industry's very own Silicon Valley - for an action packed day which will not only help you grow your business, but also inspire you and your company to aim for the moon and hit it. Guaranteed.

PARTNERSHIP

Businesses are made of people, and those people have relationships and ties to the communities they live in. Partnership is key to any business, this is where new Partnerships are created and existing ones, strengthened.

NETWORK

People are what matter most in business. And everyone is here — including C-Level — from the most influential companies in the industry, and in tech. You just can't afford to miss out on this opportunity.

INNOVATION

Get inspired by industry leaders and innovators, learn what they've already done, what they are doing now and what they are going to do in the future. Get ideas that will generate new revenue streams for you and your company.

FAQs

Where and when will CON-X be held?
It will take place on May 7th and starting at 8:30am in Son Termes, Ctra. de Palmanyola a S'Esglaieta km 1.5 in Mallorca, Spain.

How do I get there?
You can take a cab from Palma or there will be a few shuttle busses leaving from the official hotels.

How will the day play out?
Upon your arrival, a friendly TravelgateX staff member will register you and take you to the coffee spot to enjoy a delicious breakfast. Meanwhile, a member of our team will walk you through the building and introduce you to others. Every attendee will be welcomed in this fun environment and help them spark interesting conversations with other guests.
Once seated and enjoying the atmosphere, we will take a few minutes to answer all of your questions and explain the approach of the event.
Then, the first speakers will start telling their stories, how they got here, where they're going and how're they gonna do that. After each talk, there will be a 10-minute break for Q&A
Right after a lunch break, you will be seated down alongside other members in groups. During these minutes, a facilitator will go over tactical, specific and interactive topics with you.
Finally, we will wrap up the day with an afternoon party for you to take full advantage of the event and meet more people in a more relaxed and laid-back atmosphere.

What will I get with my ticket?
Each ticket includes breakfast, refreshments, lunch, conference attendance, worldwide networking, a gala dinner and after party.

How many tickets are for sale?
The entry is limited but, we will have up to 200 tickets for sale. Make sure to grab yours quickly if you wish to attend the CON-X.

Who should attend?
This conference is for you if:

  • You want to meet your Partners in a fun, relaxed and inspiring atmosphere.
  • You want to grow your business.
  • You want to connect with people who can help you push your career, business, and skillset to the next level.
  • You want to meet new people who can help you multiply your business.
  • You want to learn and get inspired by the travel industry's world leaders.
  • You love hanging out with inspiring and fun people.
  • You don't want to miss out on a trip to Mallorca.

What should I bring?
All you need is a positive attitude, be open to learn and get inspired and a few business cards in order to give to all the new people you'll meet. For everything else, we'll take care for you.
